About us

Intentional Capital is a specialty investment firm designed to serve the financial interests and impact aspirations of a select group of ultra-high-net-worth families. Our specialty is investment management with the intention of generating social and environmental impact while generating financial return. We create and implement bespoke investment strategies, enabling our clients’ investment portfolios to play a role their life’s work.

Who we are

Our clients are answering a higher call to address some of humanity’s most challenging problems with their wealth. They expect meaningful outcomes that we can measure. They are ideal long-term and strategic capital partners to growing companies whose goals are aligned with our ambitious intentions.

We are seeking impactful investments in every layer of the capital stack. Our principals and advisors have deep expertise in nurturing growing companies and in mentoring the talented leaders who build them.

Investment THESIS

The data is in. Independent research has demonstrated that sustainable investing is more than just a valid investment strategy - by many measures, it can be a superior investment strategy.

In addition to assisting our clients with manager selection and oversight for public markets, our specialty is sourcing and rigorously vetting dual-mandate investments. We seek investment-grade opportunities that also achieve positive impact in an area, such as the environment or social equality, that is of personal value to the investor.

We are actively seeking investments, both private and public, in the sectors where we believe it is possible to achieve both meaningful social and environmental impact and market-rate financial returns.

In specific cases, an investor may accept less-than-market-rate financial returns in order to support a desired social or environmental impact outcome. We believe this calculus is highly personal and investor willingness to make financial tradeoffs is highly situational. As such, we consider “impact-first” investments on a case-by-case basis.
